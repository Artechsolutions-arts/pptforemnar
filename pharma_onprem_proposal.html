How the RAG System Works
RAG ‚Äî Retrieval-Augmented Generation ‚Äî means the AI reads your actual documents to answer questions, rather than relying on memorised training data. Every answer is sourced from your files.

Chat Interface

Question is Vectorised

Local Embedding Model

The local embedding model (running on your Mac) converts your question into a mathematical "fingerprint". Your 1 TB document library is also permanently stored as fingerprints in a vector database on your server.

Instant Search Across 1 TB

The system searches all your indexed documents in milliseconds ‚Äî finding the 7 most relevant passages from any SOP, protocol, or CSR in your entire 1 TB library. It knows not just keyword matches, but conceptual similarity.

Local LLM (No Internet)

PDF Page Citations
